About Smart Event Manager

The Smart Event Manager is a web-based platform designed to streamline event management within a college environment. It serves as a bridge between administrators and students by providing a centralized space to create, share, and track upcoming events.

Our Mission

To simplify event planning and communication in colleges, ensuring that no student misses out on important academic or extracurricular activities.

Key Features

  • Event creation and management by administrators
  • Simple and clear event viewing for students
  • Categorization of events such as academic, cultural, and sports
  • Responsive design accessible on desktop and mobile devices

How It Works

  1. Admins log in and create new events with details and dates
  2. Events are published and made visible to all registered students
  3. Students log in to view, track, and stay updated on events

Benefits

For Students

Stay informed about college activities, deadlines, and opportunities in one place.

For Administrators

Easily manage event schedules, reduce confusion, and improve student participation.
